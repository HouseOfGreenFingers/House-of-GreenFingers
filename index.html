Spring Checklist

  • Transplant perenials
  • Divide mature bulb clumps
  • Prune rose bushes, apple trees, berry bushes
  • Deadhead faded flowers
  • Prepare ground - weed hoe
  • Check for aphids
  • Guide and tie climbing plants
