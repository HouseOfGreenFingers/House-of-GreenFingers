Flora and Fauna

Are Fauna always a problem? Are they Pests? Delve into what Fauna can benefit your garden.


Flora and fauna are widely associated with each other, one often relying on the other for survival. This is why it is important to understand the nature of this balance and respect it. Frequently assumed that fauna can be detrimental to a healthy garden, this is not always the case! Some Fauna can assist your garden in becoming its healthiest. Here we will take a deeper look into this association and delve into the symbotic nature of this relationship.
